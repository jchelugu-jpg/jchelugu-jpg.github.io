INDEPENDENCE DAY

THANKSGIVING

Thansgiving is fourth on my top five because I enjoy the time that I spend with my family and friends eating good food. We'll usually go out of town to visit with family friends or as a community we'll hold something for everyone.

VALENTINES DAY

Valentines Day isn't a major holiday for me but I have good memories from it from my dad and school. I enjoyed when we would have class parties and everyone would bring candy to share. My dad would get me flowers and everything else which makes the day special to me.
