TOP 5 FAVORITE GAMES

1) Bloxburg

Bloxburg is an interactive game on the main platform of Roblox. Some would consider the game a "roleplay game" but I personally enjoy playing the game for the main aspect of buildings homes.

2) Dress to Impress

This is a new game I've been recently playing with my friends. The concept of the game is to basically "Dress to Impress". You style your avatar accordingly to the category and people rate your outfit.

3) Natural Disaster

Natural Disaster is exactly what the game title says it is. There's multiple rounds where you try and survive the natural disaster such as acid rain, tornado, flooding..anything that has to do with natural diasters

4) Horrific Housing

This game is similar to Natural Disaster. The only difference is that Horrific Housing you have to be the last one standing to win.
